What this is for
Focus: confirm FTC coverage for the Memphis RPDC across the 3 USPS tours × 7 days, see exactly where you're short against the SOP station-priority model, then finalize the week and export one standalone report. The goal is a finished, shareable weekly report — not a tracker you must keep open forever.
How to use it — the 7-day process
  1. Load the roster. On the 📋 Roster tab you have three ways in: connect a shared Google Sheet (HR keeps it current — paste its link once, then hit ↻ Refresh whenever someone's hired or quits), drag in an INPUT_TEMPLATE.xlsx, or edit inline.
    Fastest per-day entry: pick from the dropdown — Off / Tour 1 / Tour 2 / Tour 3 / Avail.
  2. Review coverage. On 📊 Board & Gaps, read the 3 tours × 7 days grid. Green/Yellow/Orange/Red shows how many stations are manned; the Gaps table lists every slot below 4, worst first.
  3. Finalize each day. At the top of Board & Gaps, click a day chip once its coverage is locked (or Finalize all 7). The counter tracks X/7 until the week is done.
    This is the "walk the week" step — you confirm days deliberately, not one person at a time.
  4. (Optional) Monitor live. During a shift, use 🔴 Live Now to toggle who's physically on; it shows the current tour's live coverage and flags GAPs.
  5. Export the report. Click 📄 Export Weekly Report. You get one self-contained HTML file — open, print, email, or post it. It needs nothing installed and captures the finalized week as a snapshot.
Definitions of the 4 stations and the color scale are on the ℹ️ Legend & SOP tab. Your edits and finalized days are saved in this browser automatically.   📥 Download this guide as a PDF — print it or send it to the crew.

The 4 stations — filled in this priority order (SOP)
#1 · Inside/Outside Floor LeadALWAYS staffed 24/7/365. Runs the interior floor (primary) + exterior docks (secondary), first responder inside & out, owns driver badging / PPE / check-in. The one station that can never be empty.
#2 · Outside DocksAlways staffed. Continuous dock patrol, hourly chock + trailer audits, meets every arriving truck, collects straps, enforces driver compliance (padlock / vest / closed-toe = fines), ensures trucks unhooked & sealed before departure.
#3 · Trailer Yard / InboundOwns inbound trailer inspection (four-photo process), running yard inventory / audit, flags out-of-service units, daily technician check, moves trailers.
#4 · Overflow / SupportActivated only when a 4th body is available. Backs up #2 & #3, carrier hunting (inbound engagement), trailer decaling, owns the 3 PM / 3 AM reactivated-trailer list.
